Introduction :

Dear Students and Parents,

What an amazing show ! My students gave me the most precious gift a teacher can ask for. Each and every one of you did an outstanding job; the quality of the performance surpassed all my expectations. My mind is racing with ideas of what we can do next year; and with such a dedicated and talented group, the possibilities are endless.

I consider myself blessed to have the support of such a dedicated and efficient volunteer group. No words are enough to express my gratitude to you! My students, parents of students, friends and family all came together to make the student recital happen; allowing me to concentrate on the music itself. When I heard all the voices in the Geetham, Endaro and Thillana groups come together and soar as one, I was overcome with emotion. You made me proud and helped me realize my lifelong dream of having my students perform a Pancharatna Kriti in unison!

I hope you enjoyed the show as much I did. We at Ragamalika are constantly striving to improve and would welcome your thoughts. Please send us an email with your feedback and suggestions to raagamalika02@yahoo.com .

Regards ,
Maragatham Ramaswamy.


When and Where :

Date : 24 May 2008 Time : 4 PM to 7 PM Venue: Sri Siva Vishnu Temple, Lanham, MD (http://www.ssvt.org)
