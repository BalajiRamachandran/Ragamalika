Introduction :

Once again, Ragamalika 2004 was a roaring success because of the best volunteers in the world ! Thank you to each and every one of you for pulling off yet another concert performance with aplomb and quality ! The quality of the music, the food, the sound system, facility arrangements, stage decorations, ushers, program materials, publicity, web pages, music recording, camera work, and everything else , all came together in sweet harmony making for a great musical experience for one and all. We all left, with a great taste of music lingering on our palates and hungry for more in 2005.

The students practiced hard and were very diligent about attending rehearsals. Despite the demands on your time, made it a point to be on time both for the rehearsal on Saturday, May 22nd and the program on Sunday, May 23rd. Your dedication clearly showed in the quality of the concert!

The audience also played their part wonderfully. They were quiet, attentive and appreciative and their encouragement brought out the best from the performers.

Thank you ! Thank you ! Thank you !
