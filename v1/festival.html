Introduction :

Ragamalika 2002 was a celebration of 15 years of contribution to the field of Carnatic Music by Maragatham Ramaswamy in the DC Metro Area. Students rendered works by famous composers such as Thyagaraja, Dikshitar, Purandaradasa, among others. A sumptuous dinner catered by Arathi Cuisine was served at the completion of the program !


When and Where :

Date : 18 May 2002
Time : 3 PM to 5:30 PM
Venue: Sterling Community Center
