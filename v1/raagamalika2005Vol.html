Volunteer Roles & Responsibilities :

Facility Rental

Assigned person is responsible for facility rental. Person must make sure that we have

  •          the performance hall from 12-6
  •          find out if the additional room is available for kids to gather in

Parking Assistance

Initial parking should be facilitated at the Rajdhani Mandir Parking Lot. Overflow parking facilities are available and the assigned person(s) must call the mandir to find out the exact location of the this facility. The person(s) is also responsible for shuttling people between the Mandir and the overflow parking between 1:45 pm and 2:15 pm.

Instrument Tuning
Assigned person will ensure that all instruments are tuned and ready to go before the program starts.

Sound System

Assigned persons will be responsible for

  • setting up the sound system,
  • performing sound checks,
  • managing the sound system & adjusting mikes during the program,
  • creating an audio recording of the program
  • tearing the system down.

Ordering Food

Assigned person is responsible for ordering the cake ( with the letters 'Ragamalika' on it ).Person must purchase plates, napkins, spoons, cups, chips & salsa ahead of time and transport everything to the venue. Please also bring a knife to cut the cake.

Serving Food

As the music program is completed folks, the cake and the chips/salsa needs to be set up on a table. Assigned person will also need to bring in a tablecloth ( plastic) for the serving table.

Stage Decoration

Assigned persons will develop a plan for decorating the stage and discuss the plan with Maragatham. Setup and tear down must not take more than 45 minutes. Persons are also responsible for obtaining all required artifacts, decorations necessary.

Ragamalika Banner

Assigned persons will

  • bring the ragamalika banners to the auditorium.
  • pin the big banner to the curtain behind the stage before the program starts
  • determine a location for the other banner and put it up before the program starts
  • taking down the banners after the program.

Reception Committee

Assigned persons must arrive at 1:30 pm. Persons must

  • Setup a table with some flowers, candy, kunkumum and brochures.
  • Display collage poster board with photos from last year’s program. (Rohini Ravada will prepare poster)
  • Monitor the door. Door will open to guests at 2:15pm
  • Hand out programs as guests enter the hall.
  • Bring some paper notes for the raffle and make sure everybody writes their name on a note and puts it into the raffle box.

Arranging Chairs

Assigned persons must arrive at 1:00pm and arrange chairs for the guests. (10 seats per row ; 5 on either side with an aisle in the middle should work.) A number of rows on the left side of the aisle will be reserved for participants. (about 65 chairs).Assigned persons must also put away the chairs after the program is over.

Refreshments for Participants

Assigned person will purchase bottled water and some crackers/cookies (must not be messy) to keep the children quiet as they wait for sound checks and their turn to perform.

Stage Entry/Exit

Assigned persons must

  • Ensure that participants are seated quietly through the sound check and program.
  • Manage entry and exit of participants to and from the stage when called. Please obtain stage seating order from Maragatham for each group so participants can enter the stage in the right order.
  • You can hand out the food/refreshments brought in by Lavanya if necessary.

Program Notes

Assigned Person must design program notes, make 200 copies of the program and deliver them to the reception committee prior to the program.

Photographs

It will be wonderful if photographer can take at least one picture of each group. At 1:45 pm, participants will assemble on the stage for a group photograph. 

Video Recording

Assigned person must record the entire program from start to finish.

Cleanup Crew

Each team is responsible for setting up and taking away their own stuff. This team will chip in wherever necessary and is responsible for making sure that we cleanup and vacate on time.
