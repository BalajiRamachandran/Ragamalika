We have been informed that there may be a performance at GMU around the same time as the reception on Sunday, so traffic may tend to be backed up in and around Roanoke Lane (the closest entry point to the Center for the Arts and the Patriot Center).  In the event that occurs, these alternate directions may be handy:

 

  1. From 66:

 

Ø      Take the exit for Chain Bridge Road (Route 123) SOUTH

Ø      Make a left at the signal onto University Drive.

Ø      Make a right at the first signal onto Pohick Lane.

Ø      Make a left at the stop sign onto Patriot Circle.

Ø      Patriot Circle is a circle and you can follow signs from there to Dewberry Hall (which is within the Johnson Center).

 

  1. From 7100:

 

Ø      Take the exit for Chain Bridge Road (Route 123) NORTH

Ø      From Chain Bridge, make a right onto Braddock Rd.

Ø      At the first light, make a left onto Roanoke Lane.

Ø      Travel along Roanoke until it ends at Patriot Circle.

Ø      Make a left onto Patriot Circle and follow signs for the Johnson Center.

Ø      If Roanoke is blocked, continue straight on Braddock past it and make a left onto Roberts Rd.  From there, follow the directions in 3. below. 

 

  1. From 495:

 

Ø      Take the exit for Braddock Road WEST.

Ø      Make a right onto Roberts Road.

Ø      Make the first left (there is no stop sign or signal, but there is a church across the street) onto Shenandoah.

Ø      At the stop sign, make a left onto Patriot Circle and follow signs for the Johnson Center. 

 

  1. From Alexandria through Duke Street/Little River Turnpike:

 

Ø      Duke Street becomes Little River Turnpike (Route 236 WEST), which will become Main Street. 

Ø      From Main Street, make a left onto Roberts Road.

Ø      Travel along Roberts Road for 2-3 miles.

Ø      Make a right onto Shenandoah (there is no signal or stop sign, but there is a church across the street).

Ø      At the stop sign, make a left onto Patriot Circle and follow signs for the Johnson Center.

FROM PATRIOT CIRCLE TO THE RECEPTION:

Once you are on Patriot Circle, you have several parking options.  You can pay to park in the visitors parking deck, in which case you will need to make a right or a left onto Mason Drive (which is a one-way street) and the parking deck will be located to your right, past the Center for the Arts.

 

Otherwise, you may park in Lot K.  Lot K is located across the street from the Center for the Arts and the pond and can also be found in the map below (in the lower left corner).

 

The reception will be held in Dewberry Hall, which is in the lower level of the George W. Johnson Center (in the map on the following page, this is item number 13).
