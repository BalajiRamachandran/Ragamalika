Introduction :

Dear Students and Parents 10/30/2005

The Ragamalika student recital on November 19th is less than one week away and preparations are now in full swing! All students who have advanced beyond "Geethams" will have an opportunity to perform at this recital.

The music program has been finalized and all participating students have been told what they will be performing. Students have been working on their songs during class. If however, you have any questions about what you will be singing or playing, please contact Mrs. Ramaswamy right away.

 

When and Where :

Date: Saturday, 19 November 2005
Reporting Time: 2:00 PM
Music Program: 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM
Refreshments: 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM
Venue: Rajdhani Mandir , 4525 Pleasant Valley Rd, Chantilly VA 20151 (http://www.rajdhanimandir.org)

The show will start promptly at 3:00 pm and last for about 2 1/2 hours. The program will consist of vocal and instrumental pieces rendered by small groups of students.
All participants must be at the venue by 2:00 pm so we can start the program on time.
