Welcome, you just downloaded the most easy-to-install guestbook on earth!
You only need to have PHP installed on your webserver.
So, no need for difficult databases, etc.!

If you want to know more about PHP, take a look at this page:
http://www.php.net

Or look at my own page:
http://www.jevonweb.f2s.com


Installation:
1. Copy all the files to your webserver, everything in the SAME folder,
   for example the folder "Guestbook"
2. Chmod config.php and messages.txt to 777
   (Don't know how to chmod?? Check out this website:
    http://bruce-hamilton.com/tutorials/chmod.shtml)
3. Run the setup.php file in your favorite webbrowser, here you can configure everything you need.
4. Add links on your homepage to "form.php"(here you can add an entry)
   and "entries.php" ( here you can view all the guestbook entries))

I wish you much luck and fun with your new guestbook!
