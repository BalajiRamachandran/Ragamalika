Welcome to Ragamalika!

Ragamalika is a Carnatic music term which literally means “a garland of ragas” or musical templates. Located in Alexandria VA, Ragamalika is a premier Washington DC area music academy known for the tonal excellence and technical expertise of its students.


In 1987, Mrs Maragatham Ramaswamy set up an institution called "Ragamalika" that reflects her unique vision: to provide musical training that combines hands-on practice with theory - and above all, training vocalists to "play" their voice like a delicate instrument.

 

This website was first launched during the 15th anniversary year of Ragamalika as a tribute to a great guru and an active and lively musical organization that she leads. The website continues to be maintained by her students on a volunteer basis.

 

Classes are held weekly and over week-ends. Ragamalika students are also encouraged to participate in local musical events held at the various local venues such as the Sri Siva Vishnu Temple in Lanham, MD and the Rajdhani Mandir in Chantilly, VA.Music instruction is provided in groups. Students are placed in groups based on skill levels and availability of slots with compatible group members.

 

We look forward to seeing you, if you are not already a student. This website is designed to assist you in learning more about the learning opportunities at Ragamalika.

 

 If you are a student of Ragamalika, this website will provide support and resources and information about holidays, timings, directions and changes in timing and venues.
