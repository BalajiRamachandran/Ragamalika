Ragamalika was founded by Mrs Maragatham Ramaswamy in 1990 to provide instruction in Carnatic music vocal and violin using scientific principles and a sound grounding in voice training. Mrs Ramaswamy believes that the human voice can also be trained and operated as a musical instrument. The heart of the principle is the solid training in swara followed by clear enunciation and understanding of the sahitya.

The result of applying these principles is the startling harmony that is the hallmark of Ragamalika group performances. Regardless of the size of the group, the sound is as of one – with a sweet melody that comes from coherence in sound and training.
