Musical Discource

by

Varalakshmi Anand Kumar

 

Carnatic music is not just about singing and performing. It has a language of its own that seems to give linguists a run for their money.  “Musicalese” abounds in terms and expressions that specialize in saying every thing other than what is expressed.  A few examples of such expressions (with of course, the actual meaning alongside):

Young artist

Any one under 50 who performs

Thin audience

Nil audience

I’m a graded artist of AIR

I don’t have an A grade

 

Vocalist to his violinist two days before program

I haven’t fixed my song list yet

I’m not going to tell you my list in advance in case you practice and steal the applause

 

Artist to critic

Please come to my concert

Please give me a good review

 

In a review

A dignified performance

People were walking out in clumps

Promising artist

One of the 500 odd youngsters   desperately trying to get a foothold in the music field

Understanding support

Boring accompaniment

The concert was marked by

pleasant rendition of kritis

The artist was zero in alapanas, swara and      neraval singing

Mridanganist gave a spirited display

He was giving his instrument a sound thrashing which could be heard in the next sabha

 

Rasika to vocalist

“Margazhi” wind is affecting everybody’s throat

You sang lousily

 

 

Invitee to vocalist

You sang well

 

I’m marking attendance

You sing so well, but people just don’t know to appreciate true music

You’ll never make it in the field

 

On a bio-data

Have toured several countries like Singapore

Have toured only Singapore

An artist who blends tradition and innovation

Nothing but innovations with a Thyagaraja kriti thrown in to give respectability

 

Young artist to organizer

Thanks, Mama, for the chance

Thank you for agreeing to my persistent requests for a program

Please remember me

My last desperate appeal for a chance

 

Upcoming artist to organizer

Who are the others singing in the series?

Am I listed along with singers of repute or have I been thrown in with the amateurs?

 

Organizer to young musician

Send your bio-data and I’ll do my best

If you think you are getting a concert, you’re strongly mistaken

 

Organizer fixing main artist

Funds are running low, but we are still conducting the series for our satisfaction

You’re not going to be well paid

 

Advertisement in a sabha souvenir

Sponsored by a well wisher

Sponsored by an artist who was subsequently given a slot in December

Encouraging young talent

Providing accompanists and a hall to enable the youngster to sing outside home

We have invited as Chief Guest, Mr.XYZ, Chairman of ABC Multinational Ltd., because of his deep commitment to the art

We have invited him because we need his continued sponsorship

Rasikas welcome

Rasikas solicited

 

And finally

The future of carnatic music is safe in the hands of youngsters

Every youngster today is ready to propagate Carnatic music on stage
