Ragamalika Fundraiser 2006


Ragamalika organized a cultural program for the Amma Washington Satsang and Mata Amritanandamayi Center (M.A. Center) to raise funds towards the M.A. Center's charitable cause. Mata Amritanandamayi or Amma as she is known all over the world has been doing a tremendous service to humanity by running schools, colleges, hospitals, hospices etc. for the underpriviledged in South Asia. Her followers in the US. feed about 40,000 people a month in several locations all over the U.S.


Ragamalika's light music program included the best of Bollywood and Kollywood songs. The evening also included complimentary dinner by Amma Vegertarian Kitchen for all the attendees. The show which included live segments as well as karaoke numbers was a grand success. A writeup was featured in Express India. Amma is visiting the Washington area on July 11th and 12th.

For more information about amma please visit www.amma.org and for more information about the Washington Satsang visit www.ammadc.org
